Modern Skill

The 21st century skill where I grew the most during this project was critical thinking and problem solving. Classwork does not always go perfectly, and there were moments where I had to stop, assess what was happening, and think through the next step rather than just blindly continuing. I had to ask myself why a result looked a certain way, whether I had followed each step correctly, and what I could do differently if something seemed off. This project challenged me to think more critically, not just following instructions, but truly understanding the reasoning behind each step. That shift from simply doing to actively thinking was the most valuable thing I took away from this classroom.
